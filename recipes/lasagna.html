Lasagna

lasagna, pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Italian seasoning
  • Egg
  • Olive oil

How to Cook

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Prepare the ricotta mixture
  4. Preheat the oven
  5. Layer the lasagna
  6. Bake
  7. Rest and serve
