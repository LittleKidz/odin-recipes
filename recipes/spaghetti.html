Spaghetti

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin, typically consisting of a flattened, usually round, bread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, then baked

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes)
  • Tomato paste (optional, for richer flavor)
  • Italian seasoning (oregano, basil, thyme)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Parmesan cheese (grated, for serving)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

How to cook

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Combine pasta and sauce
  4. Serve
